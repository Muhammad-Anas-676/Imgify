FAQ

Common questions

Do I need an account to upload images?+

No. Imgify is completely account-free. Your uploads are tracked by your browser session ID stored in localStorage. Manage all your images from "My Uploads" on the same browser.

What is the maximum file size per image?+

Each image can be up to 32MB. You can upload up to 10 images at once. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, AVIF, BMP, SVG.

How long are my images stored?+

By default, images are stored permanently. You can set a URL expiry (1 hour, 7 days, 30 days, or custom) during upload. After expiry, the link shows a branded expired page.

What privacy options are available?+

Public — anyone with the link can view. Unlisted — only via direct link, not discoverable. Private — requires a password. Set during upload or change in your dashboard.

Can I use Imgify images on my website or forum?+

Yes. Imgify provides direct URLs for hotlinking, HTML embed codes, BBCode for forums, and Markdown links. All generated instantly with one-click copy buttons.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Information We Collect

Imgify does not require account registration. We collect only what is necessary: uploaded image files (temporarily for processing), anonymous browser session IDs stored in localStorage, and usage metadata (upload timestamps, image names, privacy settings, view counts).

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to host and serve your uploaded images, associate images with your browser session, and improve platform performance. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Image Storage

Images are stored via the ImgBB API. Session metadata is stored in Firebase Firestore. By uploading, you agree to ImgBB's terms regarding image storage.

4. Cookies & localStorage

We use localStorage (not traditional cookies) to store your session ID, theme preference, and cookie consent status. No tracking cookies are used.

5. Third-Party Services

We use ImgBB (image hosting), Firebase (metadata/session storage), and QRCode.js (client-side QR generation). These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You can delete any image you've uploaded via your dashboard at any time. Clearing your browser's localStorage will disassociate uploads from your browser.

Cookie Policy

What We Store

Imgify uses browser localStorage to store:

  • imgify_session — Anonymous session ID for associating uploads with your browser
  • imgify_theme — Your light/dark theme preference
  • imgify_cookie_consent — Whether you accepted or declined the cookie notice

Third-Party Services

Firebase and ImgBB may use their own cookies on their infrastructure. We do not control third-party cookies.

Managing Storage

Clear Imgify's stored data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this domain. This resets your session and theme.

Report Abuse

To report abusive or illegal content hosted on Imgify, visit the image's view page and use the "Report" button. Provide a reason and submit.

We review reports and remove violating content promptly. For urgent matters, contact us via the footer Contact link.
